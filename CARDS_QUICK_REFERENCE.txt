CARD EXPANSION SYSTEM
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

All cards are expandable (click to reveal detail section)

Expansion Classes:
  .expanded          - Applied to clicked card (shows detail)
  .card-dimmed       - Applied to other cards (blur + fade)
  .card-expanded     - Applied to body (indicates expansion active)

Expansion Handler:
  Function:  applyCardExpansion() in grid.js (lines 73-155)
  Trigger:   Card click (not on badge)
  Effect:    Detail appears below card, others get blur + desaturate
  Close:     Click card again, click outside, or press Escape

Animations:
  Card detail fade-in:    0.22s
  Card detail slide-up:   0.3s
  Distance-based blur:    Dynamic based on proximity
  Muuri layout refresh:   350ms delay
